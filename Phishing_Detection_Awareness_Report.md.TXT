# Phishing Email Detection & Awareness Report

## Introduction
Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers send fake emails to trick users into revealing sensitive information.

## Objectives
To analyze phishing emails and educate users on identifying and preventing such attacks.

## Tools Used
Public phishing datasets, email header analysis tools, and browser inspection tools were used.

## Phishing Indicators
- Spoofed sender addresses
- Fake domains
- Malicious links
- Urgent language
- Suspicious attachments

## Email Analysis
Multiple phishing and legitimate emails were analyzed and classified based on observed indicators.

## Risk Explanation
Phishing can result in data theft, financial loss, and system compromise.

## Prevention Guidelines
Users must verify emails, avoid suspicious links, and report phishing attempts.

## Conclusion
This project demonstrates practical phishing detection techniques and highlights the importance of cybersecurity awareness.
